Artefacts of the field

Artefacts are not decoration. They are consciousness made visible and are memories you can hold.

Z1N has two types of artefacts:

Live artefacts

Every live artefact carries a silent toggle:

  • At any time, you may revoke viewing rights for any holder.
  • The token stays in their wallet, but the renderer hides the memory.
  • You may restore rights whenever you wish.
  • Static artefacts remain visible forever.

The choice is always yours.

Static artefacts

Static artefacts are frozen snapshots of a single epoch.
