Properties
🔒
SoulboundNo transfer, no burn. The artefact stays on-chain forever, tied to its source Key.
✍️
InscriptionSet once at mint, max 64 bytes. Preserved permanently regardless of offering or release.
⚖️
Equal release rightsBoth maker and recipient can release the bond at any time with an optional message.
♻️
Reusable after rejectIf a recipient rejects, the artefact returns to UNSHARED and can be offered again. Reject count is recorded.
📨
Offer messageInclude an optional message (max 64 chars) with your offer. Lives in the event log only — not stored on-chain.
🏁
RELEASED is terminalOnce released, no further transitions are possible. The bond is permanently dissolved, but the artefact remains.
